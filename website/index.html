Features

Everything your team needs.
Nothing they don't.

Built for field technicians, office managers and project leads — all in one platform.

📱

QR & Barcode Scanning

Scan any asset in under 2 seconds using your phone camera. Works in the field, in the dark, and offline. No dedicated hardware needed.

🌐

Real-Time Sync Across Devices

Update an asset on the job site from your phone and see it instantly on the office dashboard. Live data, everywhere, always.

📊

Hire & Job Tracking

Know exactly which assets are out on hire, which job they're on, and when they're due back. Never double-book or lose track of gear again.

🔔

Service & Maintenance Alerts

Set service intervals, calibration dates and inspection schedules. GearOps alerts you before equipment is overdue so nothing slips through.

📄

Documents & Certificates

Attach manuals, compliance certificates, insurance docs and photos directly to each asset. Everything in one place, accessible from your phone.

🔒

Firebase-Powered Security

Enterprise-grade authentication with Firebase. Role-based access control, secure logins, and audit trails for every action in the system.

How It Works

Scan. Update.
Done.

Managing hundreds of assets doesn't have to be complicated. GearOps makes it a three-step process, every time.

1

Scan the QR sticker

Every asset has a GearOps QR sticker. Point your phone camera, the app opens the asset record instantly — no typing, no searching.

2

Update the status

Change the status to in service, on hire, under repair or in maintenance. Add notes, photos or documents right from the job site.

3

Everything syncs instantly

The change appears on every device immediately. Office managers see the update on the web dashboard in real time — no calls, no spreadsheets.
